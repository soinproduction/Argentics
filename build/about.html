ABOUT US

United by creative drive we bring the most value to our Clients. We leverage multiple years of experience in the industry to make sure that the games we make for you are enjoyed by numerous players across all the continents.

OUR STORY

We started in late 2018 as a game development company with a focus on mobile titles. Because some of our team also had solid previous background in eCommerce, shortly we branched out into gamification of applications for small to large businesses.

So now we design and develop both games and other engaging solutions for multiple platforms and industries across the globe. We've been good at doing it. After all we completed projects for brands like Audi, Nike, Volvo, Kernel and Bacardi.
